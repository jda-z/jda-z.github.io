Food & Cooking

Eating food is one of my most favorite things to do. Below is a list of some resturants I like to eat at!

The Globe

10/10

Creamy curry noodles are to die for... One of my most favorite resturants since my freshman year.

La Hacienda

9/10

Insane value if you share with others. Very good flavor, but I feel like I have tasted better tacos somewhere else. Highly recommend any of the Parrillda.

SGD DUBU SO GONG DONG TOFU & KOREAN BBQ

9/10

I love Korean food. Good soup... good bulgogi... I just think its a bit over priced, but the convenience on State Street is nice when compared to Five Star or Sol's on the Square.

Izakaya Kuroyama

7/10

Decent food, but not fully worth the price. Good hours- open late. Karaoke on Friday and Saturday nights are fun!

Here I have some foods I have cooked with my sous chef :)

Korean Kimchi Jigae and Bulgogi Chicken

Easiest and most tasty food to make. Kimchi Jigae was very forgiving to make with just 6 ingredients: kimchi, spam, onion, tofu, gochugaru, and gochujang! The Bulgogi Chicken was very easy as well. Just cook the ground chicken and add the homemade sauce.

Stir-Fry

Stir-fry is another very easy dish to cook. You just throw everything into a pan, and it turns out tasty! Here are two Thai dishes: Pad Kra Pao and Thai noodle dish.
